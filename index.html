FAQ

Quick answers.

How does MoCa find a birthday without me entering it?

It finds the "happy birthday" wishes you sent and uses the date you sent them.

Does any of my chat data leave my device?

No. Everything runs on your Mac with no network calls, no account, and nothing uploaded.

Which platforms are supported?

For now you can connect WhatsApp and Instagram, with more apps coming soon. It syncs to Apple and Google Calendar.

Will it send messages for me?

Never automatically. It reminds you and drafts a starting point. You decide what to send.
